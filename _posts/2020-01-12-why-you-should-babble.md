According to Jordan Peterson little kids babble any human phoneme (also the ones, that their parents are not capable of saying), before they learn how to speak any language. It made me think about something quite simmilar - what thoughts I am not capable to think of, because I was risen in some specific culture or speak a given language?

How limited are we because of the very structure of the language we are using, or social norms that we live in, from thinking of something revealing?

Are we restrained from creating some new mathematical theories, because they are so abstract, that they cannot be put in words, that we are using in our day to day life? Or just about something simple, that could significantly improve our lifes?

Main point of this post is to encourage you to **try to think about something, that nobody thought of before.** So, give a swing to your stiff, old mind and enjoy the rest of your day!

Thanks for reading!
