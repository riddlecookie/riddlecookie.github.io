Hi! Welcome to my blog! You’re proably not very interested in why I created this site, but I will tell you anyway – I needed some space to share my thoughts and… a rubber duck.

I’m going to write about things, that are usually not suitable for a chit chat with friends (at least without a lot of alcohol), but I just need to tell/write them somewhere, so unfortunately it fell on you.

I will try my best to give a relatable content (with references etc.), but I will also sometimes give my personal opinion on some topics, which you shouldn’t take for granted (I hope that you already know, that anything you read on the internet is not automatically true and MUST BE VERIFIED!). If you think, that I am wrong in some case, feel free to point out my ignorance in the comment section (but I’m going to defend myself!). That’s where we come to... the rubber duck. Often when you say your thoughts aloud (even to an inanimate object), they lose their original sense and don’t sound so smart anymore. Maybe sometimes during writing some post I will get a sort of revelation? Or maybe not. In that case, I hope that this blog will be read by a lot of people smarter than me, who will direct my reasoning on the right track. But I hope, that I will also open your eyes to certain matters from time to time.

So, don’t take my personal opinion for granted, write a lot of comments and just enjoy!
