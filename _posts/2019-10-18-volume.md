I woke up at 6 am in the morning. \"It's today!\" I thought. \"Time to finally move this lazy ass!\". Jumped out of the bed. Brushed my teeth. Searched the closet searching for a clean T-shirt. It's the laundry day... Took a dirty T-shirt out of the bin. It smelled bad... \"Nothing will stop me!\". I put on some old shoes and go out of the house. It's raining. \"Well, Rocky didn't give up that easily! I can be like Rocky!\".

After a little stretching I start running. Foo Fighters in my headphones. It's not that bad.

After some time my body start screaming \"hey buddy! What's going on?! What are we doing?!\". I'm ingoring this voice. I run further.

Some more time passes on, and I'm totally exhausted. \"Rambo would never give up!\" I tell myself. \"Just a little more!\". And I'm still running.

\"Just 200 more meters! 100! 50! 10! That's it! I made it!\" I tell myself after pretty impressive distance. I am really proud of myself. With a smile on my face I wait for an elevator, because I'm too tired to climb the stairs to my apartment. I take a shower, prepare some food and just go back to my daily routine.

The next thay I wake up at 7 am. Missed a couple of alarms. \"Oh shit! I overslept! But that's fine, if I hurry up, I can still complete my run before going to work.\". I'm getting up from the bed. \"Oh, oh...\" I think while falling on my face. An image of myself as Sylvester Stallone in my head changes a little bit from the one, where I'm at the top of the stairs in Philadelphia with my fists up in the air into something more like

{% include image.html url="/img/posts/2019-10-18-volume/that_was_a_great_workout.jpg" %}

\"Well, yeasterday I did some good work. I deserve some rest...\". With this in my head I go to work.

Several days pass, and my running shoes just collect more and more dust. My motivation disappeared and I returned to being lazy bum.

Sounds familiar? If you didn't notice, this posti is going to be about smart approach to make a progress in anything :P
